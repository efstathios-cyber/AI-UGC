The problem

UGC works. Producing it at scale doesn't.

Brands know UGC outperforms polished ads. The problem is it's slow, expensive, and nearly impossible to keep consistent.

💸

Creator costs don't scale

A single UGC creator delivers 4–8 videos a month at $150–$500 each. Running 10 personas across 5 niches means tens of thousands per month — before editing.

🐌

Feedback loops kill momentum

Brief → creator → revision → approval takes weeks. By the time content is ready, the trend is dead and the ad window has closed.

🎲

Quality is a lottery

Tone, pacing, script quality, and on-screen energy vary wildly between creators. Consistent brand voice at volume is nearly impossible to enforce.

⚖️

Compliance is an afterthought

Creators don't know your legal requirements. Claims that violate platform rules or regulations slip through — and you're the one liable when they do.

How it works

Brief in.
Publish-ready Reel out.

A five-stage automated pipeline takes your persona and content brief to a platform-native video — no human in the loop.

Step 01
🧠

Script generation

Forge uses your persona definition — voice, tone, niche, audience — to generate a structured script via AI. Hook, problem, agitate, solution, CTA. Platform-native pacing. No generic copy.

Step 02
🛡️

Compliance check

Before a frame is generated, the script is audited against your compliance rules. Claim violations, disallowed language, and platform policy triggers get flagged and blocked — not published.

Step 03
🎬

AI video & publish

The script ships to the video generation model with your persona's visual identity, camera style, and performance direction. The returned video passes a QA check, then lands in your publish queue.

# Configure your persona once
$ forge persona create --name "Alex" --niche fitness --tone "direct, science-backed"
 
# Run the full pipeline
$ forge generate
✓ Content pillar selected: myth_bust (Wednesday)
✓ Script generated: 38s · hook_type: provocative_claim
✓ Compliance check passed
✓ Video generation job submitted
✓ Media QA passed · reel.mp4 ready for publish
 
# Publish anywhere
$ forge publish --platform instagram tiktok
✓ Published to 2 platforms · link in bio live
Features

Everything UGC at scale requires.

Built from the ground up for volume — not one-off content creation.

🧬

Persona engine

Define any creator persona — voice, tone, visual identity, backstory, niche — and generate consistent content for it indefinitely.

✍️

AI script generation

Scripts are structured as Hook → Problem → Agitate → Solution → CTA. Platform-native pacing, specific numbers, no filler.

🎬

AI video with lip sync

Full spoken Reels with matching lip sync, natural camera movement, and consistent visual identity. No studio, no crew.

📅

Content calendar automation

The scheduler rotates content pillars across the week — education, myth bust, story, walkthrough — so your feed never feels repetitive.

⚖️

Compliance engine

Custom rule sets per brand. Rate claims, guarantees, restricted phrases — anything that fails blocks the pipeline before video generation starts.

📲

Multi-platform publish

Instagram Reels, Facebook, TikTok — publish directly from the pipeline or export a handoff package for manual review.

🔍

Media QA

Every video is inspected for identity match, script accuracy, no overlaid text, no logos, natural camera, and usable audio before publish.

⚡

Parallel generation

Run multiple personas simultaneously. 10 videos a day is the same effort as one — the pipeline scales horizontally without a cost cliff.

🧪

Mock mode for testing

Run the full pipeline locally without API keys. Mock providers simulate every stage so you can test flows without spending budget.
